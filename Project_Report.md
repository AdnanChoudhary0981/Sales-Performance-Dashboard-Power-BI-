## Executive Summary
This simulated dataset represents 3 years of transactional sales data across 4 regions. The dashboard focuses on key KPIs: Total Sales, Total Profit, Profit Margin, and Sales vs Targets.

## Key Findings (example patterns you can look for after building the report)
- Regions with highest sales: West (expected due to weighting).
- Categories: Technology and Furniture drive large ticket sales; Office Supplies have higher margins.
- Discounts above 20% correlate with lower profit margins in scatter analysis.
- Seasonal patterns: Q4 shows higher sales in many regions.

## Recommended Visuals
- KPI cards, Line chart (Sales by Month), Bar (Top Products), Map (Sales by State), Scatter (Discount vs Profit).
